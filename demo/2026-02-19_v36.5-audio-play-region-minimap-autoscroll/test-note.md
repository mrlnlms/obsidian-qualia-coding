# v36.5 — Play Region + Minimap + Auto-scroll

## What's new

- **Play region**: Click a coded region to play only that segment — no need to manually seek
- **Minimap**: Compact waveform overview with viewport indicator — click or drag for fast navigation on long files
- **Auto-scroll**: Waveform follows the playback cursor, keeping it centered in view
- **Scroll-to-seek**: Click anywhere on the timeline to jump playback to that position
- **Editable memo**: Add text annotations/memos to coded audio regions

## How to verify

1. Open `Conquerors and The World.mp3` in CodeMarker Audio
2. Create a coded region on a section of the waveform
3. Click the region — it should play only that segment
4. Check the minimap at the bottom/top — it should show a compact overview of the full waveform
5. Press play and observe auto-scroll: the waveform should follow the playback cursor
6. Click anywhere on the waveform timeline to seek to that position
7. Double-click or right-click a region to add/edit a memo annotation
