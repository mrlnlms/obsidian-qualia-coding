# v23 -- Scaffold from sample-plugin

## What's new

Fresh start for CodeMarker v2 in the code-maker_v2 vault. This version is a clean scaffold based on obsidian-sample-plugin, adapted with:

- Settings model (`CodeMarkerSettings` with `defaultColor` and `markerOpacity`)
- Settings tab (`CodeMarkerSettingTab` with color picker and opacity slider)
- New manifest ID: `obsidian-codemarker-v2`

## How to verify

1. Open the console (Ctrl+Shift+I) and check for `[CodeMarker v2] v23 loaded`
2. Open Settings > Community Plugins and confirm "CodeMarker v2" appears
3. Open the CodeMarker v2 settings tab and verify the color picker and opacity slider render
